Chicken Noodle Soup

Melt 2 tablespoons butter in large pot

Add for 5-6 min

  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 2 large carrots chopped

Stir in:

  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley

Pour in 8 cups chicken stock

Add salt & pepper to taste

Add chicken thighs

Partially cover with lid and cook for 10 minutes

Stir the noodles into the soup and cook until done (6-10 minutes)
