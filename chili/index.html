Chili

Prep

Crack open a beer

Dice 1 onion

Seed and dice 1 jalapeno

Mince 4 cloves garlic

Dice 1 green bell pepper

Grate cheese

Cook

Brown:

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • garlic
  • 1.5 tablespoons chili powder

Drain fat

Add:

  • jalapeno
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup beer
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 bouillon cube
  • bell pepper
  • kidney beans (drained & rinsed)
  • garlic
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • salt
  • pepper

Bring to boil, then simmer uncovered for 45-60 minutes
