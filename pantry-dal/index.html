Pantry Dal

Warm oil medium-low heat

Add veggies

Add:

  • 1/2 cup lentils
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 400 mL diced tomatoes
  • 400 mL coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp onion flakes
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • pepper

Increase heat to high, bring to boil, then simmer on medium uncovered

Don't forget to make rice!
